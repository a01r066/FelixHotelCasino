Introduction

Felix Hotel & Casino welcomes you to the Kingdom of Cambodia.

Felix is located in Bavet City, near the Cambodia-Vietnam border. Felix Hotel & Casino is a high quality entertainment and accommodation facility catering to local and international tourists.

We offers superior quality accommodation with a range of options from standard and deluxe rooms to deluxe and presidential suites. All room offer high-speed Internet connection, cable TV and other features.

Felix Hotel & Casino boasts a range of fine dining options prepared by our International chefs including Vietnamese, Korean, Japanese and Chinese. Our 8th floor "Sky Park" bar offers sweeping views of the Bavet area. Gamers in our main hall can enjoy our complimentary 24/7 buffet and soup station.

We offers entertainment and leisure facilities including hi-tech private Karaoke rooms, dedicated body & foot massage service, conference & meeting rooms, and duty free shopping. Our free shuttle bus offers transport around Bavet area.

Felix Hotel & Casino offers more than 70 game tables, 150 game machines and 10 Internet online games. Our main gaming hall presents a daily lucky draw and live entertainment for our guest enjoyment. Fully serviced VIP & VVIP gaming rooms with dedicated staff are available for high-rolling players.

We look forward to serving you!
