BACCARAT

Cards are dealt to the Player and Banker, and the winner is the side that is nearest to 9

GUIDELINES

Game Table

  • The player places his bet on the layout, which includes the following areas: Player, Banker, Tie, and Pair.
  • The dealer deals two cards each to the Player and the Banker.
  • The dealer opens the Player’s cards and then the Banker’s. A third card may or may not be dealt to either the Player or the Banker depending on the baccarat rules.
  • The side with the hand closest to 9 wins, and the winner receives the amount of his original bet.(1 to 1)

GAME RULES

Value of Card

  • Aces are valued at 1.
  • Kings, Queens, Jacks, and 10s are valued at 0.
  • Cards from 2 to 9 are taken at face value.
  • If the total of the cards exceeds 10, only the second digit is counted.
The winner receives the amount of the original bet.(1 to 1)

[If the Banker wins, a 5% commission is deducted]※ In no commission table, there is no deduction. However, if the banker wins with total value of 6, 50% commission will be paid to the house.

If a tie bet is won, the player receives eight times the original bet.(8 to 1)

※ Tie : the total of the Player’s and Banker’s cards are equal

If a pair bet is won, the player receives eleven times the original bet. (11 to 1)

※ Pair : the first two cards are the same value
